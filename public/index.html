Hi, I’m Alec.

A geographer and environmental scientist by training, I aspire to elevate my skillset in predictive analytics to develop insights into socio-ecological issues and build useful data-driven tools. I am currently offering a variety of GIS & data services as an independent contractor, so please feel free to contact me if you feel my skillset could help you achieve your goals.
